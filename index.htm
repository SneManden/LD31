LD31: A battle of Snow & Fire

A game by user SneManden, made for the ludumdare 31 compo, december 2014. Source code.

Tested in Chromium and Firefox for Ubuntu 14.04 (linux).

Using javascript framework phaser. Everything (code, graphics, audio, etc.) are generated within the 48 hours of the competition. Graphics drawn with Pinta Image Editor. Sounds generated with bfxr, and music with autotracker.
